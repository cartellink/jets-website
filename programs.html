Our Programs

Something for everyone — from first-timers to state competitors

JUNIORS

Junior Development

For school-age kids new to squash — no experience necessary. We'll lend you a racquet and get you started.

Fun activities, balloon squash, games-based learning. The goal is simple: give kids a great time on court so they want to come back.

Book a Trial Session

COMPETITIONS

Monthly Skill Comps

One Sunday per month at MAASH, our skill-based competitions ensure every kid gets at least three games with minimal waiting between matches.

Divisions for all ability levels — whether you're just learning to rally or competing at state level.

See Upcoming Events

TOURNAMENT

JETS Junior Open

Our annual tournament at MAASH brings together juniors from across Sydney and beyond.

Multiple divisions from beginners who are still learning to serve, right through to competitive players. A state championship with a difference — inclusive and welcoming.

ADULTS

Social & Adult Play

JETS isn't just for kids — adults of all levels are welcome.

Social pennants, casual hit-ups, and a welcoming community. Join the growing JETS family on court.
